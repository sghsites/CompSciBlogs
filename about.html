About CompSci Blogs

CompSci Blogs is a website with a selection of 3 blogs explaining different concepts for Python. The goal for this site is to teach new programmers a little bit more about python. This includes beginner mistakes, how to use variables, and creating functions.


Visual Plan

CompSci Blogs brings a minimalistic feel to the blogging scene with a complimentary coloring style. This features a dark gray sidebar with a lighter gray body. This complements each other with a distinctive contrast of both elements. We also chose a light blue for headers and white for body paragraphs, side navigation buttons, and footer because they stand out with the darker color scheme.
